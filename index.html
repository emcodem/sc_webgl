vJoy (virtual joystick) support:
• Chrome / Edge — important: if vJoy is installed, Chromium can fail to detect some of your real devices (not just vJoy — a physical stick may not appear either), and vJoy's own axes read as zero. To use a real stick in Chrome you may need to uninstall or disable vJoy. Otherwise use Firefox, which is unaffected.
• Firefox: works, including vJoy. Note that each vJoy device only appears in the list above after you move one of its axes or press one of its buttons — the browser hides it until then.
• To bind two vJoy devices to different controls, give each a different number of axes/buttons in the vJoy config — that's how they're told apart (they share one USB vendor/product ID).
